MapleLegends

Oddjobs has a guild (also called Oddjobs) that is active on MapleLegends. You can recognize us by the guild tags below our nametags:

There are absolutely no level requirements for being a member of Oddjobs. All that we ask is that you always follow a few very simple rules, any time that you are playing a character that is (or aspires to be) a member of Oddjobs:

  • Be cool. Don’t harsh anyone’s vibes; that’s not cool.
  • Be odd. Obviously, you gotta be playing an odd job.
  • Don’t leech. Don’t accept leech from anyone, and don’t leech others. Just play the game.
  • Don’t wash. HP (and MP) washing is disallowed.[1]
  • Have fun.

Oddjobs is part of an alliance by the name of Suboptimal. At the moment, the only other guild in the alliance is Flow, a largely permabeginner guild that is the de facto off-island permabeginner guild of MapleLegends. Suboptimal is, however, open to bringing more gameplay-restriction-oriented guilds into the alliance! If your guild would like to join, please do contact deer in-game (in-game names: cervid, capreolina, cervine, hydropotina) or on Discord at deer#1028!

JoblessMS

JoblessMS is a MapleStory server that is dedicated almost exclusively to odd jobs. Being based on version 62 of GMS, and having low rates, the overall framework of JoblessMS will be familiar to many. But JoblessMS is unique in its commitment to odd jobs, hence its name. The only way to play anything that isn’t an odd job is to make an “ironman” (or “iron mode”) character, which cannot be a member of parties, cannot trade with others, cannot drop items, &c., so that they are totally isolated from the rest of the playerbase. JoblessMS also offers the option to play permadeath, on a character-by-character basis.

Although JoblessMS has plenty of downsides in comparison to a more mature server like MapleLegends (particularly, the population size, of course), we recommend giving it a shot if possible. JoblessMS is the server that was responsible for the formation of Oddjobs.
